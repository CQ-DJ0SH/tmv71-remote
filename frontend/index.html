The radio's USB sound interface (full-duplex: capture = RX, playback = TX to the mic).

USB mixer levels

Hardware levels of the USB sound card (persisted). Playback drives the radio mic on TX; if it's at 0 % nothing comes out. Capture is the RX level into the Pi.

TX tones

Replaces the mic with a continuous 700 + 1900 Hz signal — for setting deviation; use a dummy load. The tone is emitted whether or not PTT is pressed; a ⚠ warning shows in the PTT panel while it is active.

TX timing

BUFFER: mic backlog cap — lower = less TX latency, higher = more tolerant of network jitter (default 250 ms). TRAIL: how long TX stays keyed after you release, so the buffered tail isn't chopped (default 250 ms).
